As a musician and a writer, Georgia Sebesky has developed an immense appreciation for storytelling. Her experience in the performing arts as well as in her journalistic pursuits has taught her how to tell a narrative via many creative modes. Georgia is currently a Journalism + Design and Contemporary Music student at The New School. She has held multiple internships and job positions including those at V Magazine, WFMU, and Opening Ceremony.
