As a musician and a writer, Georgia Sebesky has developed an immense appreciation for storytelling. Her experience in the performing arts as well as in her journalistic pursuits has taught her how to tell a narrative in several, multidemensional modes. Georgia is currently a Journalism + Design and Contemporary Music student at The New School. She has held multiple internships at corposations and organizations including V Magazine, WFMU, and Opening Ceremony. In her down time, Georgia can most often be found playing with her two cats, Olive and Iggy.
